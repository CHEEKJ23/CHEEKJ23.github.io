Ingredients Procedure
1/2 LB of Ground Beef Coat grill pan with oil then form patty. if cooking in under 10 minutes, make your patty less than an inch, if you have time you can make bigger. Once formed put on grill pan (if cooking quickly, press it down slightly). Add bacon as well as onion which should seasoned with salt, pepper and oil.
1 Egg Bun Lettuce Coat grill pan with oil then form patty. if cooking in under 10 minutes, make your patty less than an inch, if you have time you can make bigger. 3-4 Strips Bacon Form Sriracha mayo by mixing components. Add more sriracha for personal taste
